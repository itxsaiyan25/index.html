      <h1>Eren Yeager</h1>
      <p>Eren Yeager is the central protagonist—and ultimate antagonist—of Hajime Isayama's critically acclaimed manga and anime series, Attack on Titan (Shingeki no Kyojin). Driven by a relentless, fiery obsession with personal and collective freedom, his character journey remains one of the most drastic, tragic, and heavily debated transformations in modern anime history.</p>
      <section>
        <h2>Eren in AOT Season 1</h2>
        <ul>
          <li><strong>Age: </strong>10 years old in the first episode; 15 years old for the majority of the season.</li>
          <li><strong>Personality: </strong>Extremely idealistic, impatient, and fiercely protective of his friends. Unlike his calculated persona in later seasons, his Season 1 fighting style relies heavily on raw emotion, screaming, and unyielding determination.</li>
          <li><strong>The Tragedy of Shiganshina: </strong>Eren's worldview is shattered when the Colossal and Armored Titans breach Wall Maria, resulting in his mother Carla being eaten alive right before his eyes. This event solidifies his vow to wipe every last Titan from the earth.</li>
        </ul>

       <h2>Eren in AOT Season 2</h2>
       <ul>
          <li><strong>Age: </strong>15 years old in the second season.</li>
          <li><strong>Personality: </strong>Intense rage, impulsive nature, and growing feelings of powerlessness and self-doubt. While his fiery determination remains, this season highlights his frustration at being forced to rely on others and his desperation to prove his worth as a soldier.</li>
          <li><strong>Kidnapping and Rescue: </strong> After the initial breach of Wall Rose, Eren is kidnapped alongside Krista Lenz by Reiner (the Armored Titan) and Bertholdt (the Colossal Titan). The Survey Corps mounts an intense rescue mission to save him.</li>
        </ul>

       <h2>Eren in AOT Season 3</h2>
       <ul>
         <li><strong>Age: </strong>15 years old in season 3 and 16 years old in the last episode of third season.</li>
         <li><strong>Personality: </strong>A deeply conflicted, burdened, and maturing young man. He deals with heavy identity crises, insecurity, and the overwhelming realization of his father's secrets, transforming from an angry survivor into a questioning, resolute soldier.</li>
         <li><strong>The Kiss & the Future: </strong> The season's pivotal finale occurs when Eren kisses Queen Historia's hand at a royal ceremony. This physical touch triggers a traumatic and involuntary vision of his father’s memories—including glimpses of his future self and events yet to happen, which sets the stage for his dramatic change in ideology.</li>
       </ul>

        <h2>Eren in AOT The Final Season</h2>
        <ul>
          <li><strong>Age: </strong>19 years old in the final season.</li>
          <li><strong>Personality: </strong>Cold, stoic, and ruthless figure. Driven by a desperate need for freedom and burdened by the weight of the future, he becomes willing to sacrifice everything to protect his home.</li>
          <li><strong>The Founder's True Power: </strong>He unlocks the full power of the Founding Titan by utilizing the royal blood of his half-brother, Zeke Yeager, and convincing the original Founder, Ymir Fritz, to grant him her power.</li>
        </ul>
